The battle of Ligar

Whisky Company

Whisky Company was deployed on from orbit on Ligar for one simple reason, head hunting. They were the final fell swoop in a trap in which they completely encircled a clan vanguard force.

The victory was total and complete. Lance 3 of whisky company suprised the Clanner from behind and completly wiped out a star within 5 minutes, it was their finest moment in their entire battle records.

This victory achieved the following:

  1. Removal of clan artillery, forcing their units to fight without fire support.
  2. Destruction of a clan stan with no casulties.
  3. Allowed access to the rear of the frontline units.

Clan Jade falcon

A star from Jade Falcon, assigned to act as artillery for the vanguard force into Ligar. They were generally unprotected, as the clans were utterly confident that the Fedcom forcers were in a full rout, and they wished to maximize damage by deploying all combat capable unit.
